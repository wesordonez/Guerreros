Welcome to Guerrero's Pizza and Tacos! Humboldt Park's go-to spot for delicious, affordable, and satisfying fast food! For over 10 years, our family-owned restaurant has been proudly serving the community with crave-worthy pizzas, tacos, burgers, and sandwiches. Our father and son duo are passionate about creating mouthwatering dishes that cater to everyone, from young party goers to late-night munchie-seekers. We use fresh ingredients and authentic flavors to satisfy all your cravings without breaking the bank. At Guerrero's, we're all about great food, exceptional value, and a relaxed atmosphere. Swing by during our late-night hours and experience the best of Humboldt Park's culinary scene. We can't wait to welcome you into our family!
